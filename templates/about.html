03 : About me

A Fusion of Tech and Innovation

I am Kwesi Odame Danquah, a software engineer who combines academic prowess from York St John University with practical experience in Ghana's tech industry. My journey has been marked by roles that challenged and honed my skills, from system enhancements at DAAS Engineering to data management at the Food and Drugs Authority.

Mentorship and Community Involvement

Beyond my professional roles, I am passionate about mentoring in backend development, contributing to projects and guiding newcomers in the tech community. My volunteer work with 'Because She Can' in Ghana highlights my dedication to empowering future tech innovators.

Portfolio: A Symphony of Projects

My portfolio is a diverse showcase ranging from product management systems to innovative health management systems. Each project highlights my journey in the tech world, showcasing continuous learning and adaptation to new challenges in the digital landscape.
